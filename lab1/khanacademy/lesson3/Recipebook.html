Pasta Carbonara

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Spaghetti 400g
Pancetta or guanciale 150g
Eggs 3
Grated Parmesan cheese 1 cup
Black pepper To taste

Steps:

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. Saut√© pancetta or guanciale until crispy.
  3. Whisk eggs and grated Parmesan cheese.
  4. Toss cooked pasta with pancetta, then mix in the egg and cheese mixture.
  5. Season with black pepper. Serve immediately.

Grilled Chicken Salad

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 4
  • Time: 25 minutes
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Chicken breast 2
Romaine lettuce 1 head
Cherry tomatoes 1 cup
Cucumber 1
Feta cheese 1/2 cup
Balsamic vinaigrette 3 tablespoons

Steps:

  1. Season chicken breasts with salt and pepper.
  2. Grill chicken until fully cooked.
  3. Chop lettuce, tomatoes, and cucumber.
  4. Slice grilled chicken into strips.
  5. Combine all ingredients in a bowl, top with feta cheese, and drizzle with balsamic vinaigrette.
  6. Toss gently and serve immediately.

Berry Smoothie

  • Time: 10 minutes
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
Frozen mixed berries 1 cup
Banana 1
Greek yogurt 1/2 cup
Almond milk 1 cup
Honey 1 tablespoon

Steps:

  1. Combine frozen berries, banana, Greek yogurt, almond milk, and honey in a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  3. Pour into a glass and enjoy immediately.
